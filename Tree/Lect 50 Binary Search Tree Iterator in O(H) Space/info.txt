3 Types BSTIterator

1. beforeBSTIterator
- Iterator used for Reverse Inorder Traversal

2. nextBSTIterator
- Iterator used for Inorder Traversal

3. BSTIterator (Note: Either beforeBSTIterator Or nextBSTIterator But not Both)
- Iterator used for either Inorder Traversal or Reverse Inorder Traversal but not Both at a Time.
